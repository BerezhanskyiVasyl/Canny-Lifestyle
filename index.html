Yes, CBD is legal in the UK, provided it comes from an EU-approved industrial hemp strain containing less than 0.2% THC.

CBD offers health benefits such as pain and anxiety relief, improved sleep quality, and anti-inflammatory effects. It has also shown promise in addressing epilepsy, neurodegenerative disorders, and skin conditions.
